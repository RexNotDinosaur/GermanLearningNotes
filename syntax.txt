subject always conjugate the verb and in normal sentence the order is subject + verb
	e.g.
		Ich wohne in Berlin. (I live in Berlin.)
		ch bin müde. (I am tired.)


w-fragen (w-questions):
	w-word + verb + subject
	w-word:
		wie: how (not a complete correspondence)
		welche: which
		was: what
		wo: where
		woher: where from
		wie viel: how much

	e.g.
		woher kommst du?
			(where you came from?)
		wie ist dein Familienname?
			(what is your family name?)

ja/nein-fragen (yes or no):
	verb + subject + ...
	e.g.
		Wohnst du in Müchen?
			(do you live in munich?)
		Kommen sie aus Deutschland?
			(you came from germany?)
